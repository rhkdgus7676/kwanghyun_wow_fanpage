Here you will find an overview of alpha/beta data collected by Wowhead on World of Warcraft: Shadowlands, the next WoW expansion after Battle of Azeroth. This guide contains links to updates under Class and Systems, Cosmetics and Models, Collections, and Environment, as well as links to any patch notes, Wowhead Weekly updates, and Blizzard news, blogs, and interviews.

For build changes and additions, the build number appears at the beginning of the linked article (example: 33978). An article marked PREVIEW is content released by Blizzard to announce upcoming plans that have not yet been implemented. More recent news is at the top of the list.

If you are looking for an explanation of all the major content and gameplay systems in Shadowlands, including information learned at BlizzCon 2019, please visit our Shadowlands Expansion Overview.
